About Us

Since 1994, Al Bahja Olives has been your trusted source for the finest quality olives, proudly serving the vibrant community of Hay Hassani. With nearly three decades of experience, we’ve built a reputation as the go-to destination for authentic, flavorful olives that bring joy to every meal. As the #1 olive provider in Hay Hassani, we specialize in a wide variety of olives, from traditional black and green to uniquely seasoned and prepared selections. Our commitment to excellence and customer satisfaction has made us a beloved name in the neighborhood and beyond. At Al Bahja Olives, we believe in preserving tradition while offering the highest quality products. Whether for your salads, tajines, or special occasions, trust us to deliver the perfect flavors every time
